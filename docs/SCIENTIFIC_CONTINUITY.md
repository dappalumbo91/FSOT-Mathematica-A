# Scientific continuity (FSOT does not erase the path here)

## Stance

1. **The pieces that led here are already explained in the literature** — Newtonian mechanics, special/general relativity, quantum mechanics and the Standard Model, and the computational/information work that shaped this stack.  
2. **Deeper understanding comes from that prior work**, not from FSOT tokens alone.
